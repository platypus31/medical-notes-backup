# Hypoxmia（低血氧）

> [!abstract] 一句話：

## 📖 定義 / 病生理（這是什麼）
- 低血氧 A-a gradient 異常，表示氣體交換界面有異常
> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=150&selection=0,19,85,2&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.150]]
> > RESPIRATORY FAILURE
> > ![[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=150&rect=191,591,413,628&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.150]]
> > - A-a gradient = PAO2 – PaO2: normal (on room air) = “4 + age/4” or “2.5 + (0.2 × age)” 
> > - Hypoxemia + nl A-a gradient: problem is ↓ PiO2/FiO2 or ↑ PaCO2 (ie, hypoventilation)
> > - Hypoxemia + ↑ A-a gradient: problem is either 
> >     - R → L shunt, anatomic (congenital heart dis) or severe pathophys (alveoli filled w/ fluid; eg, PNA, pulm edema); cannot overcome w/ 100% O2 b/c of sigmoidal Hb-O2 curve 
> >     - V/Q mismatch where “shunt-like” areas (↓ V & nl Q) cause unoxygenated blood to mix with oxygenated blood; can be overcome w/ ↑ O2 delivery Diffusion limitation: generally seen with exercise/↑CO


![[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=150&rect=72,71,542,364|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.150]]
 - 公式：PAO2=FIO2(760-47) - 1.25PaCO2
 - PAO2=150 - 1.25PaCO2 (室內氧氣21%前提下)
 - A-a gradiant 正常 <15 mmHg
- 血氧供應(每秒供應的O2 ml)：DO2=CO(1.39HbSaO2+(0.003PaO2))
