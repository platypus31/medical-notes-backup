> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=266&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.266]]
> > - Extrarenal H2O loss (Uosm >700–800)
> >     - **GI H2O loss**: vomiting, NGT drainage, osmotic diarrhea, fistula, lactulose, malabsorption
> >     - **Insensible loss**: fever, exercise, ventilation, burns 
> > - Renal H2O loss (Uosm <700–800)
> >     - **Diuresis**: osmotic (glucose, mannitol, urea), loop diuretics
> >     - **Diabetes insipidus**
> >     - ADH deficiency (central) or resistance (nephrogenic)
> >     - **Central**: hypothalamic or posterior pituitary disease (congenital, trauma/surgery, infiltrative/IgG4); also idiopathic, hypoxic/ischemic encephalopathy (shock, Sheehan’s syndrome), anorexia, sarcoidosis, histiocytosis, drugs: EtOH, phenytoin, snake venom, tumors: craniopharyngioma, germinoma, lymphoma, leukemia, meningioma, pituitary
> >     - **Nephrogenic**: congenital (ADH receptor V2 mutation, aquaporin-2 mutation) drugs: lithium, amphotericin, demeclocycline, foscarnet, cidofovir, ifosfamide metabolic: hypercalcemia, severe hypokalemia, protein malnutrition, congenital tubulointerstitial: postobstruction, recovery phase of ATN, PKD, sickle cell, Sjögren’s, amyloid, pregnancy (placental vasopressinase) DI usually presents as severe polyuria and mild hypernatremia 
> > - Other (Uosm >700–800)
> >     - **Na overload**: hypertonic saline (eg, resuscitation w/ NaHCO3), mineralocorticoid excess
> >     - **Seizures, ↑ exercise**: ↑ intracellular osmoles → H2O shifts → transient ↑ (Na)serum

## 💊 治療（Acute / Chronic）
> 用藥 [[]]
> [!PDF|yellow] [[長庚銀蛋口袋書(印).pdf#page=45&selection=193,0,206,3&color=yellow|長庚銀蛋口袋書(印), p.45]]
> > 【Hypervolemic】
> > 治療：Hydration（D5W）+ 利尿劑
> > 【Euvolemic】
> > 原因：ADH 沒有作用，導致水分無法回收，造成高血鈉
> > 分類： 
> > 1 ）[[Central Diabetes Insipidus(中樞性尿崩症)]]
> >  病因：ADH 缺乏
> >  注射 DDAVP 後：尿液滲透壓在兩小時內增加 50%以上 
> >  治療：給予 DDAVP 
> > 2 ）[[Nephrogenic diabetes insipidus(腎源性尿崩症)]]
> > 病因：對 ADH 有抗性
> > 注射 DDAVP 後：尿液滲透壓在兩小時內增加小於 10%
> > 治療：給予 Thiazide、治療引起腎性尿崩症的原因，如[[Hypercalcemia(高血鈣)]]、鋰鹽中毒
> > 【Hypovolemic】
> > 治療：Hydration（D5W）
