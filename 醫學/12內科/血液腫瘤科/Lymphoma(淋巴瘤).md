# Lymphoma（淋巴瘤）

> [!abstract] 一句話：

## 📖 定義 / 病生理（這是什麼）
  - 周邊淋巴侵犯：
    - Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma
     - 成人：[[Diffuse large B cell lymphoma(瀰漫性大型B 細胞淋巴癌)]] & [[Follicular lymphoma(濾泡性淋巴瘤)]]
     - 小孩：[[Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia(急性淋巴性白血病)]] & [[Burkitt’s lymphoma(勃氏淋巴瘤)]]
    - Hodgkin's lymphoma
  - 骨髓侵犯：Leukemia

- Hodgkin’s lymphoma依組織學的細分類
    - Lymphocyte predominance：年輕男性，預後好(可早期診斷)，好犯頸部
    - Nodular sclerosis(70%)：年輕女性，好犯縱膈腔
- Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma依侵犯性細分類
    - Indolent：[[Follicular lymphoma(濾泡性淋巴瘤)]], [[Small Lymphocytic Lymphoma(小淋巴細胞淋巴瘤)]]/[[B cell Chronic lymphocytic leukemia(慢性淋巴細胞性白血病)]]
    - Agreessive：[[Diffuse large B cell lymphoma(瀰漫性大型B 細胞淋巴癌)]], [[Anaplastic Large Cell Lymphoma(間變性大細胞淋巴瘤)]]
    - Highly aggressive：[[Burkitt’s lymphoma(勃氏淋巴瘤)]], Adult T cell lymphoma/leukemia, [[Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia(急性淋巴性白血病)]]

Ann Arbor staging system

| 分期  | 特徵(RS cell即是Clonal B cell) |
| --- | -------------------------- |
| I   | 侵犯單一淋巴結                    |
| II  | 侵犯>2個淋巴結(但在同側橫膈膜)          |
| III | 侵犯淋巴結在兩個橫膈膜都有              |
| IV  | 侵犯到淋巴器官以外                  |

International Prognostic Index (IPI) for NHL(Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma)

五項指標(APLES)：Age>60，Performance status>2 (ECOG) or >70 (Karnofsky)，LDH > normal，>1 site of extranodal involvement，Ann Arbor stage III or IV
