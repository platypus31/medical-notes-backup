## ⭐ 特徵 / 注意（pearls & pitfalls，易漏易考）
- [[Superior Vena Cava syndrome(上腔靜脈症候群)]]
  - 惡性腫瘤(肺(SCLC & Squmous cell lung cancer佔85%原因)/淋巴/轉移癌)佔90%原因
  - 治療：利尿劑/頭抬高/給O2/Clucocorticoid對Lymphoma有幫助
  - [[Non-small cell lung cancer(非小細胞肺癌)]], Metastases：radiation therapy
  - [[Lymphoma(淋巴瘤)]], [[Small Cell Lung Cancer(小細胞肺癌)]]：chemotherapy
- [[Tumor lysis syndrome(腫瘤溶解症候群)]]
  - 通常是high grade lymphoma(eg. [[Burkitt’s lymphoma(勃氏淋巴瘤)]])或Leukemia([[Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia(急性淋巴性白血病)]])，solid tumor少見
  - 臨床表現：血鉀上升/Uric acid上升/血中PO4上升/血鈣下降/腎衰竭(Urate nephropathy)
  - 治療：Allopurinol+IV hydration/NaHCO3鹼化尿液+利尿劑/Rasburicase (clear uric acid from the blood)可在腎衰竭情況下降低uric acid
- [[Spinal cord compression(脊髓壓迫)]]
  - [[Prostate Cancer(攝護腺癌)]]/[[Breast Cancer(乳癌)]]/[[Lung Cancer(肺癌)]]常見，以胸椎最常見
  - 臨床表現：痛/肌肉無力/自主神經功能喪失(尿失禁等)/感覺喪失
  - 診斷：Urgent MRI
  - 治療：懷疑,確診前給steroid/Emergent radiaton or surgical decompression
- [[Hypercalcemia(高血鈣)]](Humoral hypercalcemia of malignancy)
  - PTHrP：鱗狀細胞癌(肺癌/頭頸部/食道/皮膚)/乳癌/泌尿系統癌症
  - 1,25(OH)2D：[[Lymphoma(淋巴瘤)]]
  - Osteolytic cytokines, inflammatory mediators：[[Breast Cancer(乳癌)]]/[[Multiple myeloma(多發性骨髓瘤)]]骨髓癌
  - 治療：  
    - 生理食鹽水靜脈輸注+利尿劑(Loop diuretics)
    - Bisphophomates：有腎毒性，可能造成Jaw osteonecrosis
    - Dialysis
    - Calcitonin
    - Glucocorticoid：對lymphoma/leukemia/myeloma造成的高血鈣有效
