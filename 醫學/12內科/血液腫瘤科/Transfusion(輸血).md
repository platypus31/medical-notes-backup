> 用藥 [[]]

|                            | 適應症/內容物                                                                   | 備註                                                                                           |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| packed RBC                 | Hb <7 or <10 with bleeding                                                | 輸2U可上升1g/dL(0.5~0.7g/dL)<br><br>*美國輸1U可上升1g/dL左右                                             |
| Platelets                  | Plt < 10k or < 20k with infection or bleeding risk or < 50k with bleeding | 輸6U可提升30k<br><br>禁用於TTP/HIT/HELLP syndrome                                                   |
| Fresh frozen plasma(FFP)   | 含所有凝血因子                                                                   | 可用於DIC/TTP/肝衰竭                                                                               |
| Cryoprecipitate<br>（冷凍沉澱品） | 含Fibrinogen/vWF/Factor 8/Factor 13                                        | 可用於[[Renal Failure(腎衰竭)]]/vWD/[[Hemophilia(血友病)]]A型病患出血時                                                 |
| Irradiated RBC/Plt         | Lymphocyte inactivation(血品照光)                                             | 防止transfusion related GVHD，immunocompromised p’t用<br><br>一日BMT終身照光                           |
| Filter RBC/Plt             | 過濾器濾掉大部分WBC                                                               | 濾掉Lymphocyte → 減少GVHD<br>濾掉Granulocyte → 減少CMV感染                                             |
| Leukoreduced               | 減白血品，儲存前已拿掉WBC                                                            | 儲存前比輸血前過濾更減少血中cytokine                                                                       |
| Washed RBC                 | Plasma完全以N/S取代（洗滌紅血球）                                                     | 可減少血品中plasma中的抗體，幾乎將WBC及cytokine去除，IgA deficient recipients or other anaphylactic reactions用 |
