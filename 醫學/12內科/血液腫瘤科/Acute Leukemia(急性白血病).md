## ⭐ 特徵 / 注意（pearls & pitfalls，易漏易考）
[![Lymphoma. ALLMMCLLLymphomas Hematopoietic stem cell Neutrophils Eosinophils  Basophils Monocytes Platelets Red cells Myeloid progenitor  Myeloproliferative. - ppt download](https://images.slideplayer.com/22/6345103/slides/slide_2.jpg)

- 癌症基因型
  - [[Lymphoplasmacytoid lymphoma(淋巴漿細胞型)]]：t(9;14)
  - [[Mantle cell lymphoma(被套區細胞淋巴瘤)]]：t(11;14), oncogene=cyclin D1(BCL-1)
  - [[Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia(慢性淋巴性白血病)]]/[[Small Lymphocytic Lymphoma(小淋巴細胞淋巴瘤)]]：t(14;15)
  - [[Follicular lymphoma(濾泡性淋巴瘤)]]：t(14;18), oncogene=BCL-2
  - [[Burkitt’s lymphoma(勃氏淋巴瘤)]]/leukemia：t(8;14),t(8;22),t(2;8), oncogene=C-MYC
  - [[Precursor B cell Lymphoblastic Leukemia(B細胞前驅因子淋巴白血病)]]：t(4;11), oncogene=AF4,ALLI
  - [[Hairy cell leukemia(毛细胞白血病)]]：Tartratr-resistant acid phosphatase(+)

- peroxidase>3%: AML MPO(+) = M2/M3/M4

- 表面抗原
  - CD19(+)/CD5(+): [[Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia(慢性淋巴性白血病)]], [[Mantle cell lymphoma(被套區細胞淋巴瘤)]]
  - CD19(+)/CD10(+): [[Follicular lymphoma(濾泡性淋巴瘤)]]ar
  - CD19(+)/CD25(+): [[Hairy cell leukemia(毛细胞白血病)]]
