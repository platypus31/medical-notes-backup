# Ventricular Tachycardia(心室性心搏過速)

> [!abstract] 一句話：三個以上連續出現的 PVC、速率 120~200/分的寬 QRS 心搏過速，依 QRS 是否一致分單形性/多形性，寬 QRS tachycardia 中約 80% 為 VT。

## 🎯 判讀步驟（rate → rhythm → axis → P → PR → QRS → ST/T → QT）
- **Rhythm**：規則（monomorphic）或不規則（polymorphic）的寬 QRS 心搏過速
- **QRS**：寬（monomorphic 每一跳形態相似；polymorphic 逐跳改變）
- **P 波**：AV dissociation（獨立的 P 波、capture / fusion beat）可證實為 VT
- **Axis**：extreme axis deviation 傾向 VT
- **Rate**：每分鐘 120~200 次
- **定義**：三個以上連續出現的 [[Premature Ventricular Contractions(室性早搏)]]

## 🔑 關鍵特徵（要辨別的 pattern / 診斷準則）
> [!PDF|yellow] [[長庚銀蛋口袋書(印).pdf#page=137&color=yellow|長庚銀蛋口袋書(印), p.137]]
> > - 定義：三個以上連續出現的 PVC
> > - 速率：每分鐘 120~200 次
> > - 病因： 
> >     - 1 ）併有結構性心臟病（VT associated with structural heart disease ） 
> >     大部分 VT 發生在有結構性心臟病的病患，尤其是[[Ischemic Heart Disease(缺血性心臟病)]]。 
> >     曾經梗塞的區域附近（Peri-infarct）或疤痕（Scar）容易形成 reentry，導致 monomorphic VT 
> >     缺血區域（Ischemic）常與 polymorphic VT、[[醫學/11心電圖判讀/Ventricular fibrillation(心室纖維顫動)]] 有關
> >     [[Cardiomyopathy(心肌症)]]、[[Arrhythmogenic Right Ventricular Dysplasia(誘發心律不整之右心室心肌病變)]]等也與 VT 有關 
> >     - 2 ）無結構性心臟病（VT in the absence of structural heart disease ）
> >     可能是先天心肌上離子通道的問題，如 [[Brugada syndrome(布魯蓋達氏症候群)]]、[[Long-QT syndrome(長QT症候群)]] 等，都可能導致 polymorphic VT 
> >     排除上述問題，有些人是 idiopathic VT，放電點常在 right ventricular outflow tract（RVOT）， 較少見的還有 LVOT VT、fascicular VT 
> >    - 分類與治療： 
> >      - 1 ）單形性 VT （Monomorphic VT ） 
> >      出現 Unstable sign 一律電擊：（同步電擊）單相或雙相 100 焦耳，若無反應再逐步增加劑量 
> >      若穩定，可用 [[Amiodarone]] 治療 
> >      Amiodarone 劑量： 
> >      Loading dose ：150mg IVF run 10 mins
> >      Maintain dose ：1 mg/min x 6 hrs, then 0.5 mg/min
> >      Amiodarone 泡法：
> >      Loading dose ：1PC in D5W 50cc run 10 mins
> >      Maintain dose ：6PC in D5W 500cc 玻璃瓶 run 33 ml/hr x 6 hrs, then 17 ml/hr
> >      治療[[Acute Myocardial infarction(急性心肌梗塞)]]後 VT：Lidocaine（IV）或 Mexiletine（PO）
> >      治療[[Digoxin Intoxication(毛地黃中毒)]]引起的 VT（Bidirectional VT）：Phenytoin（IV） 
> >      - 2 ）多形性 VT （Polymorphic VT ）
> >      出現 Unstable sign 一律電擊： （非同步電擊）單相 360 焦耳，雙相 120~200 焦耳（去顫劑量）
> >      若穩定，可用 [[Amiodarone]] 治療（劑量與泡法同上） 
> >      - 3 ）無脈搏性 VT （Pulseless VT ） 
> >      CPR + 電擊（ACLS 流程）： （非同步電擊）單相 360 焦耳，雙相 120~200 焦耳（去顫劑量）
> ![[長庚銀蛋口袋書(印).pdf#page=138&rect=53,251,529,495&color=yellow|長庚銀蛋口袋書(印), p.138]]

> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=85&selection=98,0,133,18&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.85]]
> > - ECG features that favor VT
> > - AV dissociation (independent P waves, capture or fusion beats) proves VT
> > - Very wide QRS (>140 ms in RBBB-type or >160 in LBBB-type); extreme axis deviation 
> > - QRS morphology atypical for BBB (longest precordial RS >100 ms and R wider than S) 
> >     - RBBB-type: absence of tall R′ (or presence of monophasic R) in V1, r/S ratio <1 in V6 
> >     - LBBB-type: onset to nadir >60 ms in V1, q wave in V6 
> > - Initial R wave in aVR; concordance (QRS in all precordial leads w/ same pattern/direction)

## 🧩 對應疾病（這個 pattern 指向什麼 → 連科別）
> 心臟內科

> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=84&selection=172,0,230,59&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.84]]
> > Monomorphic ventricular tachycardia (MMVT)
> > - All beats look similar; predominantly upward in V1 = [[Right Bundle Branch Block(右束支傳導阻滯)]]-type vs. downward = [[Left Bundle Branch Block(左束支傳導阻滯)]]-type
> > - In obviously structurally abnormal heart: prior MI (scar); [[Cardiomyopathy(心肌症)]]
> > - In apparently nl heart that is actually diseased: subtle [[Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy(肥厚型心肌病)]], infiltrative [[Cardiomyopathy(心肌症)]], [[Myocarditis(心肌炎)]], arrhythmogenic CMP (ACM): incomplete [[Right Bundle Branch Block(右束支傳導阻滯)]], ε wave (terminal notch in QRS) & TWI in V1–V3 on resting ECG LBBB-type VT, dx w/ MRI 
> > - In structurally normal heart w/ normal resting ECG & cardiac MRI: RVOT VT: LBBB-type VT or PVCs w/ inferior axis; typically ablate LVOT VT: RBBB-type VT or PVCs w/ superior axis; responds to verapamil
> > ![[截圖 2025-03-01 上午9.29.29.png]]

> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=85&selection=0,0,64,43&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.85]]
> > Polymorphic ventricular tachycardia (PMVT)
> > - QRS morphology changes from beat to beat
> > - Etiologies: ischemia; CMP; catecholaminergic 
> >     - [[醫學/11心電圖判讀/Torsades de Pointes(多型性心室心搏過速)]] (TdP = “twisting of the points,” PMVT + ↑ QT): ↑ QT acquired (meds, lytes, [[Stroke(中風)]]) w/ risk ↑ w/ ↓ HR, freq [[Premature Ventricular Contractions(室性早搏)]] (pause dependent) or congenital (K/Na channelopathies) w/ resting Tw abnl & TdP triggered by sympathetic stimulation (eg, exercise, emotion, sudden loud noises) 
> >     - [[Brugada syndrome(布魯蓋達氏症候群)]] (Na channelopathy): ♂ > ♀; pseudo-RBBB w/ STE in V1–V3 (provoked w/ class IA or IC) on resting ECG
