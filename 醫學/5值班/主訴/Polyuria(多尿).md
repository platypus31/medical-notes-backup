## - 筆記

- 症狀

- 診斷
  - 分類
    - [[Central Diabetes Insipidus(中樞性尿崩症)]]：通常和腦垂腺創傷、手術、[[Hypoxmia(低血氧)]]等有關係
    - [[Nephrogenic diabetes insipidus(腎源性尿崩症)]]：要注意藥物史(遺傳性、鋰鹽)
    - [[Primary Polydipsia(原發性多飲症)]]：在中年女性較多，常合併其他精神疾病
    - Osmotic 造成的多尿：[[Diabetes Mellitus(糖尿病)]](吃多喝多尿多、體重減輕)
![[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=269&rect=87,432,530,726&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.269]]


  - 先確定是否有脫水的狀況
  - PE：黏膜、capillary refill time(正常小於 2 秒)、[[Orthostatic Hypotension(姿勢性低血壓)]]、skin turgor
  - 檢查是否有其他 [[Panhypopituitarism(全腦垂體機能低下症)]] 的問題、意識狀態
  - 後續檢查：抽血、尿液 osmolarity、給 DDAVP後的反應

- 治療
> [!PDF|note] [[Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition.pdf#page=269&selection=2,0,57,5&color=note|Pocket Medicine The Massachusetts General Hospital Handbook of Internal Medicine, 8th Edition, p.269]]
> > - 1° polydipsia: treat psychiatric illness, check meds, restrict access to free H2O
> > - Osmotic diuresis: address underlying cause, replace free H2O deficit (see “Hypernatremia” for formula to calculate) and ongoing losses
> > - DI: 
> >     - Central DI: desmopressin (dDAVP, 1st line), low Na/protein diet + HCTZ, chlorpropamide 
> >     - Nephrogenic DI: treat underlying cause if possible; Na restriction + HCTZ (mild volume depletion → ↓ delivery of filtrate for free H2O absorption), consider amiloride for Li- induced DI (Kid Int 2009;76:44), indomethacin (NEJM 1991;324:850) or trial desmopression 
> >     - Pregnancy-induced DI: due to vasopressinase from placenta, ∴ Rx w/ dDAVP
