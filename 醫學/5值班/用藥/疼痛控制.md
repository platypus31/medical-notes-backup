## - 術後止痛
- acetaminophen/paracetamol
		- ceiling effect: 1000mg oral
		- dosage: 
		    oral: paracetamol 500mg/tablet, max 4g/day
		    iv: paracetamol 1g/amp, max 8g/day
		- administered with NSASID on a scheduled basis
		- 注意肝毒性
	- tramdol
		- dual mechanisam: weak u agonist + SNRI
		- metabolized by cytochrome CYP2D6
		- 可能導致seizure
		- 避免與SSRI併用
	- pregabalin/ gabapentin
	     - alpha 2 delta ligands
	     - 主要用在神經痛，減少中樞的敏感度central sensitisation
	     - ﻿﻿Latest evidence: 沒證據支持術後疼痛控制的使用
			- ﻿﻿In a meta-analysis of 281 RCTs comparing gabapentinoids with controls, no clinically meaningful difference in acute or chronic pain was observed. The adverse events of dizziness and visual disturbance were greater with gabapentinoids use.
			- ﻿﻿These results do not support the routine use of pregabalin or gabapentin for the management of postoperative pain in adult patients.
	- dexmeditomidine
		- alpha-2 agonist
		- clonidine (Catapres): anti-hypertensive drug口服的是一個降血壓藥
		- Disadvantages: hypotension, bradycardia and dose-dependent sedation
		- dexmeditomidine 健保給付規範：限用於**短期可拔管需鎮靜**之18歲以上加護病房病人使用，連續使用不得超過24小時，再次使用需間隔6小時以上，每次住院最多使用3次。
	- corticosteroids
		- anti-inflammation effect
		- Ongoing debate: the potential risks of hyperglycemia, wound infection, bleeding and possibly **malignancy recurrence**
		- 通常是用在手術中，防止噁心嘔吐的角色
	- lidocaine
		- continuous iv 
		- 也是抗心律不整用藥，所以小心cardiac risk
		- LAST: local anesthetic systemic toxicity
		- evidence is ucertain
		- transdermal 用在長期疼痛的病人
	- NMDA Antagonists: ﻿﻿Ketamine
		- low-dose infusion
		- opioid-sparing effect, decreasing opioid side effects
		- reducing development of opioid-induced hyperalgesia
		- reducing chronic surgical pain
	- NMDA Antagonists:MgSO4
		- perioperative infusion
	- NMDA Antagonists:Methadone
		- not routinely use in postoperative pain management
